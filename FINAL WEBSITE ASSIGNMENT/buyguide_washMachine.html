Washing Machine Buying Guide



Washers and dryers might require a heavy investment but the comfort and ease they offer definitely make them worth the price. Washing machines not only ensure a safe and hygienic wash, they also help us save a lot of time and effort. This guide will simplify the process of buying a washing machine so that you can pick the best one for your clothes.

SELECT THE RIGHT CAPACITY

Before you set out to buy a washing machine, it is important to understand the maximum weight of clothes your machine can accommodate per cycle. This capacity usually ranges from 5-10 kg per cycle.

TYPES OF WASHING MACHINES

WASH PROGRAMS

Wash programs save you from random guesswork by suggesting the right wash treatment for different types of clothes and fabrics.

EXCITING FEATURES

  • The RPM Choice :- The machine will remove water from your clothes faster with a high RPM. This is particularly useful during the monsoon season.
  • Quick Wash :- The Quick Wash mode comes in handy when you need to urgently wash a few clothes without setting any wash programs.
  • Heaters :- Detergents work better in eliminating stubborn dirt, oil and microbes at higher temperatures.
  • Fuzzy Logic :- This mode helps the washing machine automatically pick the right wash cycle based on the load weight and dirt. It is also capable of calculating the exact amount of soap and water required for the wash cycle. Useful and efficient for new users!
  • Bubble Wash :- This mode produces bubbles that go deep into the fabric to ensure that your clothes are washed in superior quality. By offering the same wash quality as heaters, it saves you from exorbitant electricity bills.
  • LCD Display :- Larger displays are clearer and easier to read. They also display more information like the time left for your wash cycle to finish.
