Laptop Buying Guide



Whether it's business or entertainment, laptop is a go-to for many specialised tasks. Outshining the regular desktop in terms of portability and several other functions, laptops are of utmost importance to many programmers, gamers, professionals and Internet users. While purchasing a laptop, it is advisable to pick one that befits your computing needs. Here are a few factors that you should consider before picking your laptop.

PERFORMANCE

One of the most important things to note while buying a laptop is its performance. Your laptop should completely match your needs and requirements with the kind of power you are looking for.

  • Your laptop's ability to handle multiple programs and activities depends on the speed of the processor. This means that your laptop will work faster if the speed of the processor is higher.
  • RAM determines how smooth and lag-free your laptop will be.
  • The higher the RAM, the better it is.
  • You can enjoy a lag free experience with a minimum of 4GB RAM.

WHO SHOULD CONSIDER SSDs?

Even though SSD-storage-based laptops offer low storage capacity, they offer 5x the performance benefits in comparison to standard HDD based storage. You should consider SSDs when:

  • Working with large files (>1GB)
  • Looking for a laptop that boots quickly
  • You don't need require a lot of storage space
  • Need a pure performance laptop
  • Laptops with SSD storage offers better battery backup as compared to HDD laptops
  • Laptops consisting SSD storage are lightweight and compact as compared to laptops carrying HDD storage
