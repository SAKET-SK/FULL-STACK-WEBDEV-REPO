Television Buying Guide



Buying a new TV can be a confusing and complicated task considering the wide variety of TVs available in the market. Modern TVs come with a plethora of functions and features. Thus, it is important to understand what amplifies your watching experience. While some might look at the size or resolution, others might fancy exceptional sound quality. Let's look at a few aspects closely so that you know exactly what you want when you purchase your TV.

SCREEN SIZE

  • Up To 81 cm (32) :- If you primarily watch HD content (720p), go for an 81 cm (32) TV or one with a smaller screen. It is advisable to maintain a viewing distance of at least 5 feet to enjoy the best viewing experience.
  • 81 cm (32) - 127 cm (50) :- If Full HD content (1080p) from DTH services and Blu-ray players is something you like to watch on TV, make sure you choose one with a screen size between 81 cm (32) and 127 cm (50). Maintain a minimum viewing distance of 6 feet at least.
  • Above 127 cm (50) :- The ultimate way to enjoy 4K/Ultra HD content is on a TV of screen size above 127 cm (50). In this case, a minimum viewing distance of 8 feet will offer the best watching experience. Considering that 4K content is not widely available, make sure that your content sources are in place.

BACKLIGHT & SCREEN TYPE

  • The visuals on your screen light up because of the backlight (flashlight) in TVs.
  • An ideal setting prevents the visuals from getting too dim or too bright depending on the room's lighting.
  • In a sun-drenched room, a brighter backlight fights the sun's light and reduces glare.
  • In a dimly lit room, darker backlight prevents your eyes from straining while watching the Television screen.
  • Types - LED, Backlight Dimming & Edge lit lights. These lights are used in different types of screens.
